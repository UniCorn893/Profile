DEEPFAKE AI DETECTION AND RESTORATION

Currently, I'm engaged in a project focused on developing a deepfake detection and restoration system. Utilizing cutting-edge artificial intelligence techniques, our project aims to identify manipulated images and videos created through deepfake technology, a sophisticated method of digitally altering media to portray false events or narratives.

By leveraging advanced algorithms and neural networks, our system can discern subtle visual cues indicative of deepfake manipulation, enabling us to restore authenticity to morphed media and combat the spread of misinformation in digital content.
